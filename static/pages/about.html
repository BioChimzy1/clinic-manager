About ClinicManager

Built on one clinic's real problems, not a boardroom's idea of them.

ClinicManager started as a tool for one clinic to stop losing track of stock, cash, and patients between a notebook, a cashbox, and memory. What works there is what's in here — nothing more.

Why it exists

Most clinic software is built for hospitals with IT departments and budgets to match. Small clinics and pharmacies — the ones actually running on a notebook and a calculator — get left out, or sold something built for a problem they don't have.

ClinicManager goes the other way. It was written for a single, real clinic's daily operations first: registering a walk-in, deducting the right batch of medicine, recording a partial payment with a witness when a patient can't pay in full that day. The features exist because a clinic floor needed them, not because a spec sheet asked for them.

What's inside
  • ✓Patient registration and a single live queue
  • ✓Appointment scheduling and doctor review
  • ✓FEFO inventory — oldest stock leaves first
  • ✓Price history with a record of who changed what
  • ✓Cashier flow: full payment, discount, or loan
  • ✓Over-the-counter retail sales
  • ✓Finance reports by day, week, or month
